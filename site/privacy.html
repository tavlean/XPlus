Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2025

Overview

XweetTab is a browser extension that enhances your Twitter/X experience by allowing you to open posts in new tabs. This privacy policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data Collection

XweetTab does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension:

  • Only runs on twitter.com and x.com domains
  • Does not access your Twitter/X account or personal information
  • Does not track your browsing activity
  • Does not send data to external servers
  • Only modifies how links open on Twitter/X pages

Permissions

The extension requires minimal permissions:

  • activeTab - To modify the current Twitter/X page
  • https://twitter.com/* - To work on Twitter
  • https://x.com/* - To work on X (formerly Twitter)

Third-Party Services

XweetTab does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

Open Source

This extension is open source. You can review the complete source code on GitHub.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on GitHub.
