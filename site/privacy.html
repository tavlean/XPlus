Privacy Policy

Last updated: Sep 25, 2025

Overview

XPlus is a browser extension that enhances your Twitter/X experience by allowing you to open posts in new tabs. This privacy policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data Collection

XPlus does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension:

  • Only runs on x.com domain
  • Does not access your X account or personal information
  • Does not track your browsing activity
  • Does not send data to external servers
  • Only stores your feature preferences and snooze settings locally in your browser
  • Only modifies how links open and redirects on X pages

Permissions

The extension requires minimal permissions:

  • tabs - To open new tabs for posts and notifications
  • storage - To save your feature preferences and snooze settings locally
  • alarms - To handle automatic re-enable after snooze periods
  • declarativeNetRequest - To redirect home page to bookmarks
  • https://x.com/* - To work on X (formerly Twitter)

Third-Party Services

XweetTab does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

Open Source

This extension is open source. You can review the complete source code on GitHub.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on GitHub.
