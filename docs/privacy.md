# Privacy Policy

XPlus runs locally in your browser. It does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

### What XPlus does

- Detects when you are on X (formerly Twitter).
- Applies your chosen focus and utility settings to that page, for example opening timestamps in new tabs or redirecting the home feed.
- Stores your settings in your browser's extension storage.

### Data XPlus does not collect

- No personally identifiable information.
- No browsing history.
- No analytics.
- No tracking.

XPlus does not contact any external server. Your settings stay in your browser's extension storage and are never sent anywhere.

### Permissions

XPlus requests only the permissions it needs to modify pages on X, save settings, manage focus breaks, and show local notifications. Permissions are used solely for that purpose.

### Changes

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, the changes will be reflected here on this page.
